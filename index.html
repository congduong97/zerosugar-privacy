Privacy Policy

Zero Sugar Challenge · Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Zero Sugar Challenge ("the app") is a habit-tracking app that helps you track your zero-sugar journey. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

The app collects the following data, which you provide voluntarily:

  • Name – optional, used for personalization (e.g., greeting)
  • Challenge data – check-in dates, duration, notes per day, reminder settings
  • Language preference – English or Vietnamese

3. Storage

All data is stored locally on your device only. We use MMKV (a local storage solution) – no data is sent to any external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising networks.

5. Notifications

The app uses local notifications (expo-notifications) to remind you to check in. Notification scheduling is done entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings → "Start new challenge". This removes your name, challenge progress, and notes from the app.

7. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
