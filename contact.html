Zero Sugar – Support & Privacy

Zero Sugar is a simple habit tracker that helps you complete sugar‑free challenges. Set a challenge length, check in once per day, and track your streak on a clean calendar.

How to Use Zero Sugar

1. Start a challenge

  • Choose a challenge duration (for example 7, 14, or 30 days).
  • Confirm your start date to begin.

2. Check in each day

  • Open the app once a day.
  • Mark the day as Success or Failed.
  • Optionally add a note about how you felt or what you ate.

3. Track your streak

  • Use the Tracking screen to see successful, failed, and upcoming days.
  • Watch your streak grow and see how long you’ve stayed sugar‑free.

4. Turn on reminders (optional)

  • Go to the Settings tab.
  • Enable daily notifications so you don’t forget to check in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app track calories or specific foods?

No. Zero Sugar focuses only on the habit of avoiding added sugar. You simply log whether you stayed on track each day.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can use the app immediately after installing it. There is no registration, login, or password.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. All core features work offline because your data is stored locally on your device.

What happens if I delete the app?

All of your locally stored data (challenges, check‑ins, and notes) will be removed from your device when you uninstall the app.

Privacy Policy

1. Data we collect

  • The app stores your challenge information, check‑ins, and notes locally on your device.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers inside the app.
  • We do not send your check‑in data to external servers for analytics or advertising.

2. How your data is used

  • Your data is used only to display your challenges, progress, and notes inside the app.
  • If you enable notifications, the app uses your device’s local notification system to send reminder alerts.

3. Data sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising.
  • We do not use third‑party analytics SDKs inside the app to track your behavior.

4. Data storage and security

  • All challenge data is stored locally on your device’s storage.
  • Because there is no account system, your data is tied only to your device. If you lose or reset your device, your data may be lost unless you use system‑level backups (such as iCloud or Google backup).

5. Children’s privacy

Zero Sugar is intended for general audiences and is not specifically targeted at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to this policy

If the privacy policy changes in the future, this page will be updated to describe what data is collected and how it is used.
